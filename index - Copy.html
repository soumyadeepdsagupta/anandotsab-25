About Anandotsab '25

Join us for the 9th year of celebrating the grandeur of Durga Puja in Bellandur, Bangalore, organized by the Green Glen Layout Cultural Association (GGLCA), an initiative by Prachesha Socio Cultural Trust. This 5-day extravaganza, held at Sobha Lakeview Clubhouse, recreates the magic of Kolkata’s Durga Puja with electrifying cultural programs, gastronomic delights, and community bonding.

Anandotsab attracts over 30,000 unique visitors from 50+ apartment complexes, offering a culturally diverse and inclusive celebration. Featuring music, dance, drama, drawings, and endless food options, it’s more than a festival—it’s a link to our roots.

Demographics

OCR: "Anandotsab 2024 - Audience Age Distribution: 25-45 (60%), Families, Professionals, Students, 30,000+ Visitors, 50+ Apartment Complexes, Bellandur, Bangalore"

The festival draws a cosmopolitan crowd, with the majority aged between 25 and 45, representing families, working professionals, and students from Bangalore’s IT corridor. Over 30,000 unique visitors from more than 50 apartment complexes participate, reflecting high purchasing power and a keen interest in cultural experiences. Brands benefit from direct engagement with a vibrant, urban audience seeking authentic celebrations and community connection.

2024 Accolades

OCR: "Anandotsab 2024 - Award Ceremony: 20+ Cultural Excellence Awards, Innovative Pandal Design, Community Engagement, Recognized by Local Media and Cultural Bodies"

Anandotsab 2024 was honored with over 20 awards for outstanding cultural programming, creative pandal designs, and impactful community engagement. The organizing team’s dedication was recognized by local cultural organizations and media, celebrating their innovation and inclusivity. These accolades reinforce Anandotsab’s reputation as a benchmark for Durga Puja celebrations in Bangalore, inspiring future events and fostering pride within the community.
