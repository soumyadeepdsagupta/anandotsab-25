About Anandotsab '25

Join us for the 9th year of celebrating the grandeur of Durga Puja in Bellandur, Bangalore, organized by the Green Glen Layout Cultural Association (GGLCA), an initiative by Prachesha Socio Cultural Trust. This 5-day extravaganza, held at Sobha Lakeview Clubhouse, recreates the magic of Kolkata’s Durga Puja with electrifying cultural programs, gastronomic delights, and community bonding.

Anandotsab attracts over 30,000 unique visitors from 50+ apartment complexes, offering a culturally diverse and inclusive celebration. Featuring music, dance, drama, drawings, and endless food options, it’s more than a festival—it’s a link to our roots.

Our Achievements

Who Are We

The Green Glen Layout Cultural Association (GGLCA), under Prachesha Socio Cultural Trust, is a cornerstone of cultural unity in Bangalore. Since its founding, GGLCA has been dedicated to preserving Bengali traditions while fostering community spirit through vibrant festivals like Anandotsab. Our team of passionate organizers and volunteers works tirelessly to create an inclusive, memorable experience that celebrates heritage and brings people together.

Demographics

Anandotsab attracts a diverse audience of over 30,000 unique visitors from Bellandur and surrounding areas, with 60% aged 25–45, including professionals, families, and students. This demographic, residing in over 50 apartment complexes, boasts high purchasing power and a deep appreciation for cultural events. Located in Bangalore’s IT hub, our festival offers brands a unique opportunity to engage with a vibrant, culturally inclined community seeking authentic experiences.

2024 Accolades

Anandotsab 2024 was a landmark year, earning over 20 prestigious awards for cultural excellence, innovative pandal designs, and exceptional community engagement. Our dedicated team of organizers and volunteers received accolades from local cultural bodies and media outlets for their creativity, inclusivity, and flawless execution. These awards highlight our commitment to delivering a world-class Durga Puja experience that resonates with attendees and sets a benchmark for cultural festivals in Bangalore.
