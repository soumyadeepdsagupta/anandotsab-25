About Anandotsab '25

Join us for the 9th year of celebrating the grandeur of Durga Puja in Bellandur, Bangalore, organized by the Green Glen Layout Cultural Association (GGLCA), an initiative by Prachesha Socio Cultural Trust. This 5-day extravaganza at Sobha Lakeview Clubhouse recreates the magic of Kolkata’s Durga Puja with electrifying cultural programs, gastronomic delights, and community bonding. Anandotsab attracts over 30,000 unique visitors from 50+ apartment complexes, offering a culturally diverse and inclusive celebration featuring music, dance, drama, drawings, and endless food options—more than a festival, it’s a link to our roots.
